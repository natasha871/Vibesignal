
# VibeSignal

## Build fast. Validate with humans.

VibeSignal connects AI builders and indie hackers with real humans who test products before launch.

Get structured feedback on:
- onboarding
- UX friction
- unclear positioning
- bugs
- mobile experience
- first impressions

---

## Why

AI makes building easier.

Human validation becomes more valuable.

Most AI-built products today are:
- self-tested
- AI-tested
- friend-tested

VibeSignal helps builders get real-world human signal before shipping.

---

## What we're building

- Human feedback loops
- Structured product testing
- Feedback threads between builders and testers
- Video-based reviews
- Launch confidence for AI products
